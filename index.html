Twenty-Five Years of Fine Home Building & Remodeling

Manos Construction has achieved an excellent reputation in the home building industry in the Greater Seattle area.

At Manos Construction we combine the highest standards of workmanship in the building industry, with maintaining a competitive cost for our customers.
