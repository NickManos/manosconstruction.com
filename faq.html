Frequently asked questions:

What are the goals of Manos Construction in a building project?

We believe the customer should receive the following:

  1. Excellent service, quality craftsmanship, and a well managed job.
  2. A project that is completed within budget and on time.
  3. The project will achieve the goals envisioned, which may include an addition of space, but certainly includes an increase in the convenience and beauty of the home, thereby improving the quality of life of our customers.
  4. Our customers will feel their vision was realized, they received good value, and we were good custodians of their money.
  5. The appreciation of a job well done that allows our customers to recommend Manos Construction with confidence.

When should Manos Construction become involved in our project?

In general we find it beneficial for the customer if we are involved as early as is practical in the project. Besides building rapport between members of the project team, our technical assistance and willingness to estimate various options for our clients helps insure you get a top quality job within your budget.

How does Manos Construction financially manage a project?

We use three main tools: estimates, job budgets, and change orders.

We start with a detailed estimate, which many architects have described as being among the most thorough they have seen.

Next a job budget is created, which breaks the costs of a project into many different categories.

As a job progresses, costs are compared to this budget on a weekly basis.

Whenever there is a change in the scope of the work, a change order is created for the customer’s review.

These measures as well as our expertise in building have resulted in our reputation, as stated by many customers, of being “good custodians of their money.” We are proud of the fact that, in more than 18 years of business, we have a fine record of accuracy in the estimating of project costs.
